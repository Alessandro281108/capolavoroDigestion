THIRD STAGE
The Stomach and Digestion
Digestion in the stomach is a complex process involving several phases and components. When food is ingested, it passes through the esophagus and enters the stomach, where the digestive process begins. The walls of the stomach contain glands that secrete gastric juices, which are primarily composed of hydrochloric acid and digestive enzymes such as pepsin.
