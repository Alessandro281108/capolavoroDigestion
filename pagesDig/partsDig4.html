DIGESTION
FOURTH STAGE
Intestinal Absorption
Intestinal absorption is the phase of the digestive process that allows the body to obtain the energy it needs to grow and maintain itself.
Intestinal absorption occurs in the small and large intestine which is approximately 7 meters long in total.
The small intestine completes digestion that began in the mouth and continues in the stomach.
